GoatHub was built by four Olin students for Codestellation, a hackathon at Brandeis University. Prior to constructing GoatHub, none of its creators had any web development experience. GoatHub was made with a sole purpose: to provide the world with an immersive and interactive goat-viewing experience.

We hope you enjoy the site as much as we enjoyed making it!
